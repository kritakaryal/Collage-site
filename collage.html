About Collage

Established in 1989 /2046 BS 'Horizon GBS College' has now been firmly grounded across the nation as a brand. NHC is a renowned institution for higher education that offers a diverse range of courses to students seeking academic excellence. Located in the the college provides a conducive learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty members. With its emphasis on holistic development and industry-focused curriculum, Horizon GBS College is truly a stepping stone towards a successful and fulfilling career. The main motto of our institution is to nurture cultured children full of confidence with level based skills and motivation to the next step in their education who care about the individuals, community and the nation.

We are proud of our academic performance but, education here means so much more than the pursuit of top grades. Sports, arts, community services, adventurous activities and so on are all central to balance the program we offer. School imparts disciplined and moral value based education which grows its students to be ethical, respectful to others and responsible citizen of the nation.

One of the key highlights of Horizon GBS College is its wide range of courses catering to different disciplines. From business administration to computer science, the college offers programs that are designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge required to excel in their chosen field. The courses are meticulously crafted to meet the demands of the industry, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the challenges they may face in the real world.

Furthermore, Horizon GBS College places great importance on practical learning and industry exposure. The college organizes regular workshops, guest lectures, and seminars that provide students with valuable insights from industry experts. Additionally, students are encouraged to undertake internships and projects with leading organizations, which not only enhances their practical skills but also exposes them to real-life work scenarios. This focus on practical learning sets Horizon GBS College apart from other institutions and contributes to the overall development of its students.

In conclusion, Horizon GBS College's comprehensive range of courses, dedicated faculty, and emphasis on practical learning makes it a sought-after destination for students aspiring to pursue higher education. our college provides an enriching environment where students can learn, explore, and grow. With its focus on holistic development, quality education, and a nurturing atmosphere, our college strives to be a place where students can discover their true potential and graduate as confident and successful individuals.With its industry-focused curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities, Horizon GBS College provides a platform for students to realize their potential and embark on a successful career journey.
